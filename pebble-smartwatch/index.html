Pebble Smartwatch

Pebble smartwatch projects

ShoOne

A simple watchface ShoOne watchface for Pebble Smartwatch.

This watchface is tailored to my taste. Analog watch with no second handle, rounded and distinguishable minute and hour handles, day of the week, day of the month, bluetooth connection vibration alert and display, battery and battery percentage, digital time display on the bottom, 3 and 9 o’clock digits for readability, so everything I need. Probably everything you will need and like.

(Was) Available in Pebble Appstore (Free download)

 

ShoMaurice

A more classical-looking watchface, ShoMaurice for Pebble Smartwatch.

This watchface is, again, tailored to my taste. Analog watch with no second handle, distinguishable minute and hour handles, day of the week, day of the month, bluetooth connection display, battery and battery percentage, nicer fonts, so everything I need. Probably everything you will need and like.

(Was) Available in Pebble Appstore (Free download)
