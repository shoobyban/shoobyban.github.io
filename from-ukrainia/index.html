From Ukraine

Beregszasz (Beregovo), Ukraine, Dormitory of Transcarpatian Hungarian College Named After Ferenc Rakoczi II… tomorrow we will give speeches on free software for teachers.

I’m sitting in front of my machine, after a long trip. We had a meeting in Nyiregyhaza with the others and some pizza then in a 5 cars long convoy we started to annoy the border guards ðŸ™‚ It was a dumb situation when the Ukrainian border guard asked us (of course in Ukrainian language) about why we are waiting in a forbidden area. This was after the nearly half hour process of filling silly papers (immigration card, LOL)… Brr… We have seen writings about getting out of cars are forbidden, so we were scared a bit ðŸ™‚

The weather is … well it’s everything but not nice. At Budapest we had sunshine and hot summer. We did not prepare with warm clothes, so we will have to buy some if the rain will not stop soon…

Back to the wine…
