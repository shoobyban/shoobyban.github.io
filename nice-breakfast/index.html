Nice breakfast

 04/12/2004 -  Sam Ban

The sandwich was fine (with chorizo), Emese had a melange (she said it was much more like melange compared to the others she had) and we are both using our own WiFi network from home, working and surfing… It’s a beautiful day, good start.
