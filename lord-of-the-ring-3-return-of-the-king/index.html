Lord of the Ring 3 – Return of the King

 16/01/2004 -  Sam Ban

OK, yesterday evening I managed to collect my friends and buy tickets for

LOTR3. I enjoyed it, the film had outstanding visual effects – except the

hobbits… The bluebox stuff was lame and badly lighted. The storyline was

well made, not much quake-like bloodbath… I think the giant spider was a

bit too scarry. The end was long, but they left out the last boring events

from the book, so it was a bit hollywood-kind end (family, kids, happy wife

:))

It was too long, 3 hours and 10 or 20 minutes… My legs was numbed, I don’t

like sitting much like that.

Eight from ten.
